Cognitive Behavioral Therapy Worksheet

After a situation, people often have automatic thoughts that are distorted

Once identified, the thought is examined

This type of worksheet helps a person identify, analyze, and change unhelpful thoughts and behaviors.

ex.


01. Situation : Failed a test
02. Automatic thought : “Im stupid.”
03. Emotion : Sad (80%)
04. Evidence for thought : I failed this test
05. Evidence against thought : Passed previous exams
06. Balanced thought : “One test doesnt define my ability.”
07. Outcome : Sadness reduced to 40% :)
